Customer Help Centre

How It Works

  1. Browse available bags from University of Liverpool Guild vendors
  2. Reserve here, then pay in person
  3. Arrive during the collection window shown on the bag
  4. Pick up your bag and enjoy your discounted food

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve a bag?

Go to the Dashboard and browse available bags. Click on a bag to view details, then press Reserve to complete payment.

Where do I see my reservations?

Your reserved bags appear in the Reserved section, along with collection times.

What do I do when I arrive to collect?

Go to the vendor during the pickup window and show your reservation confirmation. Then proceed to make your payment at their till.

What if I miss my collection time?

If you don’t arrive within the pickup window, the vendor may relist or dispose of the bag. Refunds are not guaranteed.

Tips for Customers

  • Arrive on time for your collection window
  • Check allergen information before reserving
  • Have your confirmation page ready when collecting
