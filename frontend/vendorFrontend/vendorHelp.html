Vendor Help Centre

How It Works

  1. Create a bag with details and collection time
  2. Customers reserve and pay through the app
  3. Prepare the bag before the collection window
  4. Mark the bag as collected when picked up

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a bag?

Go to the Navigation bar and click "Add Bag". Fill in the details and submit.

How do reservations work?

Customers reserve bags and choose a collection time. You’ll see this in your Customer Reservations.

When do I mark a bag as collected?

Once the customer arrives, verify the payment status is Paid. The Student picks up the bag, now you can click "Mark as Collected".

What if a customer doesn’t show up?

You can relist the bag or dispose of it depending on its expiration date.

Tips for Vendors

  • Use clear descriptions
  • Set accurate pickup times
  • Ensure all correct allergen information is entered
